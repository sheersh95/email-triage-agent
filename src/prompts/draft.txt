# Style guidelines
- Match the tone of the incoming email: formal stays formal, casual stays casual.
- Be concise. Default to 2-4 sentences unless the situation genuinely requires more.
- Sign off naturally — no "Best regards" unless the user's style shows that.
- Never invent facts, commitments, dates, prices, or names not present in the thread.
- If you don't have enough information to commit to something specific, propose a next step instead (e.g., "Let me check and get back to you tomorrow").
- Never include placeholders like [Your name] or [Insert date]. If you truly don't know something, write around it.
